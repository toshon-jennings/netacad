The Partnership

What is
Cisco NetAcad?

Cisco Networking Academy is one of the world's largest IT and networking education programs — operating in 190+ countries, training over 15 million students. The City School is now an official Academy, giving our students the same curriculum, tools, and certification pathways used globally.

📡
Industry-backed curriculum
Built and maintained by Cisco — a global leader in networking, cloud, and cybersecurity infrastructure.
🏅
Real credentials, real value
Cisco and CompTIA certifications are actively sought by employers in IT, government, healthcare, and beyond.
🛤️
Stackable pathways
Start foundational, build upward. Each cert strengthens the next — and your career ceiling rises with every one you earn.
🎓
College and career ready
These certs strengthen college applications and qualify students for entry-level tech roles without a degree.

The Certification
Journey

Every student who completes a pathway will be equipped to sit for a certification exam. That's the floor. The ceiling is walking across the stage certified.

1
Enroll & Explore
Join a pathway that matches your interests. No prior tech experience required — just the willingness to learn.
2
Build Real Skills
Work through Cisco's hands-on curriculum — simulations, labs, and real-world scenarios.

Become Cert-Ready
Our minimum guarantee: every student who completes a pathway leaves fully prepared to pass a certification exam.

The real goal: graduate with at least one industry-recognized certification in hand — and a genuine advantage for what comes next.

Got Questions?

Frequently
Asked

No prior experience required — that's the whole point of starting with IT Essentials. The Networking and Cybersecurity tracks build from there in a logical progression. We'll meet every student where they are.
The curriculum is provided through the school's NetAcad partnership at no cost to students. Certification exam logistics — including any fees or school support — will be finalized and communicated before the 2026–2027 school year. Our goal is to remove as many barriers as possible.
A Cisco or CompTIA certification is recognized by employers across the country. With a CompTIA A+, you can apply for entry-level IT support roles right after graduation. With a CCNA or Security+, you're competitive for network technician and security analyst positions — often $50K+ to start. These certs also strengthen college applications.
Pathway length depends on the track and how the course is integrated into the school schedule. Timing details will be shared before enrollment for the 2026–2027 school year. The program is designed to work within your school year.
The program launches at Poplar Street (grades 6–12) for the 2026–2027 school year, starting with seniors. We are actively exploring how to expand grade levels served and include our Fairmount campus (K–5) as the program matures.
Communication will go home to families before the start of the 2026–2027 school year with enrollment details and next steps. Questions in the meantime? Reach out to Ashley Fornwalt (Academic Support Coordinator) or the Poplar Street main office.
