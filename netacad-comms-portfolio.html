Hi team, I wanted to share a full picture of where we are with the Cisco Networking Academy partnership and what it means for our students heading into next school year. As you may know, The City School is now an official Cisco NetAcad Academy. That's not a small thing. View Standalone Version Dear Parent or Guardian, I am writing to share an important new opportunity that will be available to your student beginning in the 2026–2027 school year. The City School is now an official partner of Cisco Networking Academy (NetAcad) — one of the largest and most respected information technology education programs in the world, serving students in more than 190 countries. Through this partnership, students at our Poplar Street campus will have access to three structured technology pathways, each designed to prepare them for an industry-recognized certification exam before graduation. THE THREE PATHWAYS Pathway 1 — IT Essentials Students will build foundational knowledge of computer hardware, operating systems, and technical troubleshooting. Completing this pathway prepares students to pursue the CompTIA A+ certification, a nationally recognized credential for entry-level information technology roles. Pathway 2 — Networking Students will develop an understanding of how computer networks and the internet function, including routing, switching, and cloud infrastructure. Completing this pathway prepares students to pursue the Cisco CCNA and CompTIA Network+ certifications. Pathway 3 — Cybersecurity Students will learn to identify and respond to digital threats, protect networked systems, and understand security operations. Completing this pathway prepares students to pursue the Cisco CyberOps Associate and CompTIA Security+ certifications — among the most sought-after credentials in the technology sector today. No prior technology experience is required for enrollment. These pathways are designed to be accessible to all students regardless of their technical background. OUR COMMITMENT Our goal is that every student who participates in a pathway will be fully prepared to sit for a certification exam upon completion. Our deeper goal is that students graduate with at least one industry-recognized certification in hand — a credential that is valued by employers across industries and strengthens applications to colleges and technical programs alike. WHO IS ELIGIBLE For the 2026–2027 school year, this program is open to seniors in the Class of 2027 at Poplar Street. We intend to expand availability to additional grade levels in future school years as the program grows. NEXT STEPS Detailed enrollment information and next steps will be communicated directly to families of rising seniors before the start of the 2026–2027 school year. In the meantime, you are welcome to visit our program page for more information: [INSERT WEBPAGE URL] If you have questions, please contact Ashley Fornwalt, Academic Support Coordinator, or reach us at the Poplar Street main office. We are happy to answer any questions you may have. We are proud to offer this opportunity to our students and their families, and we look forward to sharing more with you in the months ahead. Sincerely, _________________________________ [John Deaton / Krista Wieder] [Principal / Head of Schools] The City School — Poplar Street Campus Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
