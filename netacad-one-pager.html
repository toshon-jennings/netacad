What is Cisco NetAcad?

Cisco Networking Academy is one of the world's largest IT education programs — 190+ countries, 15+ million students, 30+ years. The City School is now an official Academy, giving our students access to the same curriculum, tools, and certification pathways used by programs around the world. Credentials earned here are recognized by employers nationally and strengthen both college applications and direct career entry.
